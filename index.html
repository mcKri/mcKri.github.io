who/why is this guy?

Hi, I'm some Dutch guy who's been absorbed by video game music for very long.
After around 12 years of playing very cool instruments (mostly drums, guitar, bass), I've finally made steps in the past couple of years to make my own stuff. This started off with making chiptunes in FamiTracker and Beepbox.co, I've always found minimalistic retro sounds very charming!

I'm not the most knowledgeable musician on earth, but I really enjoy finding the right sound to add some more life to specific scenes!

I started working on a (mostly) solo game project, solely as an excuse to make more game music.
